About Us


 Bright International School is a private international school with branches in Maradana, Kolonnawa and Kandy.

 The schools are managed by Colombo Institute of Studies and headed by the chairman, Mr Zufer Ahamed.

 The schools have been in operation since the year 2009 and are approved by the Ministry of Education.

 There is a total student population of approximately 1000 students.

Our Aim
